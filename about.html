Managing Director - Festus Kyalo Mutua

Festus Kyalo Mutua leads GreenFuture Kemawasoc Company with a vision for a Green future.

Professional Background

Festus began his career at Compassion International as a Project Director. His expertise spans across various domains including Financial management and Information Systems Management.

Education

Festus holds a Bachelors Degree in Education from Egerton University, and he is currently pursuing a Masters Degree in Business Administration (MIS Option). His strong academic background is complemented by numerous professional certifications in finance and investment management.

Community Involvement

Festus is also deeply committed to giving back to the community. He is actively involved in supporting needy students in secondary schools in Kitui county, where he contributes his time, finances and expertise to support fees payment.
